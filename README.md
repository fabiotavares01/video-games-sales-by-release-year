## Overview
An interactive Power BI report analyzing global video games sales by release year,
covering data from 1980 to 2016 across multiple regions, publishers, video games, platforms and genres.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel
- Power Query

## Dataset
Source: [Video Game Sales - Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/gregorut/videogamesales)

## Data Preparation
- Data cleaning and manipulation done in Power Query
- Handled NULL values in Year, Genre and Publisher columns
  through manual research to ensure data accuracy

## What Was Built
- Multi-page dashboard covering Publishers, Games, Platforms and Genres
- Regional sales breakdown across North America (NA), Europe (EU), Japan (JP) and Other markets
- Dynamic filtering by release year range (1980–2016)

## Key Insights
- Nintendo leads all publishers with nearly 2,000M in total sales
- North America accounts for the largest share of global sales (4,389M)
- Sales declined after 2008, with the only exception being 2013
  where the annual sales growth rate recovered at 1.14%
- Top 3 games by sales on release year: Wii Sports (1st), Grand Theft Auto V (2nd)
  and Super Mario Bros (3rd)
- PS2 was the platform with the most sales across all years
- Action was the best-selling genre across all years
